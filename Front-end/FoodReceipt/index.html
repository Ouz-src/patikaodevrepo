...Chocolate Cubes...

A flavour with chocolate and pistachio... Only use 4 materials to prepare this magnificent chocolate cubes. Cubes which can be served with colored papers or with
a toothpick is especially the choice of crowded tables and activities like birthday party.

For how many people: 4 | Preparation time: 30 minutes | Cook time: 10 minutes

Materials for Chocolate Cubes Receipt
  • 350 gr dark chocolate
  • 150 ml cream
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 150 gr pistachio (almond or hazelnut can be used instead)
How to prepare Chocolate Cubes
  1. Melt the bitter chocolate with bain-marie method.
  2. Add cream and butter to the molten chocolate, mix them up and put the pot to counter
  3. Pestle the pistachio to smaller pieces.
  4. Add the crumbly pistachio pieces to pot and mix all of them.
  5. Pour the mixture you prepared to a aluminum tray if you have one or use small tray with a oiled paper.
  6. Keep it in refrigerator for 2 hours.
  7. Remove the chocolate from mold and cut it to square pieces.

Enjoy your meal!
